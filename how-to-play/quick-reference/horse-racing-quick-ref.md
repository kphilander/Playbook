# Horse Racing — Quick Reference

## How it works
Pari-mutuel betting: all bets go into a pool, the track takes a percentage (the "takeout"), and the rest is split among winners. You're not betting against the house — you're splitting the pool with other bettors after the track's cut.

## The key numbers
- **Win/Place/Show takeout:** `15-18%`
- **Exacta/Quinella:** `20-22%`
- **Trifecta/Superfecta:** `22-25%`
- **Pick 6/multi-race:** `20-30%`

## One thing to know
Simple bets give back more. A Win bet returns about `$82-$85` per `$100` wagered over time. A Trifecta returns about `$75-$78`. The excitement of exotic bets comes at a mathematical cost — a bigger takeout from the pool.

## The math in plain English
The takeout is the track's built-in margin. On a Win bet with `15%` takeout, for every `$100` wagered across all bettors, `$85` goes back to winners. Exotic bets (exacta, trifecta) carry higher takeouts — typically `20-25%`. The odds on the board shift right up until post time as more money enters the pool.
