# Baccarat — Quick Reference

## How it works
Pick Banker, Player, or Tie. Two hands are dealt. The hand closest to `9` wins. The dealing rules are automatic — you make no decisions after the bet. That's the whole game.

## The key numbers
- **Banker bet:** `1.06%` house edge (after `5%` commission)
- **Player bet:** `1.24%` house edge
- **Tie bet:** `14.4%` house edge — more than 13 times worse than Banker

## One thing to know
The Banker bet has the best odds on the table. The `5%` commission on wins sounds like a catch, but even after commission, Banker at `1.06%` beats Player at `1.24%` and crushes the Tie bet at `14.4%`.

## The math in plain English
On Banker bets, for every `$100` you bet over time, you'd lose about `$1.06`. On Tie bets, that same `$100` costs you `$14.40`. Same table, same game, very different math.
