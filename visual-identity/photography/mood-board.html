Subject Categories
Social / Group

Friends at events, shared experiences, genuine connection. Groups of 2–6, leaning in, laughing, engaged. Sports bars, rooftops, living rooms.

friends gathering social event group fun diverse candid warm lighting
Sports / Entertainment

Stadium energy, watch parties, fan culture. Focus on the crowd experience, not athletes. Wide establishing shots + medium crowd reactions.

sports fans stadium crowd watch party cheering high-five excited
Mobile / Digital

Casual phone use in everyday settings. Quick glance, relaxed posture, one-handed hold. Phone use is incidental, not obsessive. Café, commute, park.

person phone casual checking phone relaxed coffee shop smiling quick glance
Casino Environment

Social, energetic, upscale. Groups together, dressed up, bright colorful lighting. Never dim, never seedy, never solitary players at machines.

casino social entertainment venue upscale bright group fun nightlife

Do / Don't
Do

Show real, diverse people having a genuine good time together. Warm lighting, social moments, candid body language.

Solitary figure, dim room, cold light
Don't

Show distressed, solitary, or desperate-looking subjects. No dark rooms, empty wallets, or head-in-hands imagery.

Do

Use candid, editorial-quality photography that feels authentic. Real moments, real settings, natural expressions.

Generic, over-posed stock photo
Don't

Use generic, lifeless stock photography that looks like a compliance department created it. No forced smiles or staged setups.

Do

Show casino environments as bright, social, energetic spaces. Groups together, well-lit, upscale entertainment.

Close-up of chips, money, solo player
Don't

Glamorize high-stakes play. No close-ups of large chip stacks, cash, or betting slips. No solitary players at machines.
