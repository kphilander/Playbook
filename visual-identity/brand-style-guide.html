Game IQ

A) Higher than normal — you're "due" for a win
B) Exactly the same as every other spin   ✓
C) Lower than normal — the machine is "cold"
D) Depends on how much you believe in yourself

Voice & Tone

The confident myth-buster. The Insider who knows the game and respects you enough to give it straight.

Deposit Limit Prompt

Playbook voice

"Set your deposit limit. It takes 10 seconds. Go on, we'll wait."

Compliance voice

"In accordance with responsible gaming policies, players may elect to establish voluntary deposit restriction parameters via account settings."

Odds Education

Playbook voice

"Every game has a house edge — it's how casinos work. Knowing the edge helps you pick your games and set your budget."

Compliance voice

"Players should be aware that gambling outcomes are determined by random number generation and that the mathematical expectation is negative."

Session Awareness

Playbook voice

"You've been playing for 2 hours. Most sessions average about 45 minutes. Set a session reminder?"

Compliance voice

"Pursuant to regulatory requirements, this notification is to inform you that you have been actively engaged in gambling for a period of 2 hours."

Helpline Display

Playbook voice

"Free, confidential support — for any question about gambling. Call 1-800-522-4700, text 800522, or chat at ncpgambling.org/chat."

Compliance voice

"If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, call 1-800-522-4700."

Language Guide

Words matter. These swaps transform compliance copy into content players actually engage with.

Use this Instead of this Why
Player Gambler Inclusive and neutral
Smart play Responsible gambling Aspirational, not judgmental
Tools / features Interventions / measures Choice, not imposition
Your limits Restrictions Ownership, not control
Take a break Self-exclude Temporary and voluntary
Know your game Be aware of the risks Empowerment, not warning
Session reminder Reality check Helpful, not patronizing
