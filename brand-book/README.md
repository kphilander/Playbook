## How to use this

- **New to Playbook?** Read chapters 0-2 first to understand the brand foundations.
- **Writing copy?** Chapters 4 and 5 are your primary references for voice, tone, and messaging.
- **Designing assets?** Chapter 3 covers the visual system, with detailed specs in [`visual-identity/`](../visual-identity/).
- **Deploying to a new market?** Chapter 7 (application) and chapter 8 (governance) plus the [`jurisdictions/`](../jurisdictions/) modules.
