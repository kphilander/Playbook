Surveys Pick one

Pulse

3 items · <1 min · 30 responses
  • NPS (0–10): Would you recommend?
  • Forgettable–Memorable (1–7)
  • One thing you'd change? (open)

Full

7 items · ~2 min · 30 responses (60 for A/B)
  • NPS (0–10)
  • 5 semantic pairs (1–7 each):
    Forgettable–Memorable · Boring–Engaging
    Preachy–Respectful · Confusing–Clear
    Generic–Made for me
  • Open-ended feedback

Recall

4 items · 7–14 days after · 25 responses
  • Free recall: what do you remember?
  • 2 true/false knowledge checks
  • Behavioral follow-through checklist
