Instruments

Three surveys. Pick one.

All grounded in validated marketing research methodology (Reichheld 2003, Osgood et al. 1957, Dillard et al. 2007). No research expertise required.

Pulse

3 items · under 1 min · 30 responses
  • NPS (0–10): Would you recommend?
  • Forgettable–Memorable (1–7)
  • Open-ended: one thing you'd change

Full

7 items · ~2 min · 30 responses
  • NPS (0–10)
  • Forgettable–Memorable
  • Boring–Engaging
  • Preachy–Respectful
  • Confusing–Clear
  • Generic–Made for me
  • Open-ended feedback

Recall

4 items · 7–14 days later · 25 responses
  • Free recall: what do you remember?
  • True/false knowledge check #1
  • True/false knowledge check #2
  • Behavioral follow-through checklist
