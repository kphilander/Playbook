Playbook Scope -- What Is (and Isn't) Included

Playbook Covers
  • Player education content (game guides, odds tables)
  • Responsible gambling messaging (74 core messages)
  • Helpline display and crisis support templates
  • Self-exclusion communication templates
  • Limit-setting promotional messaging and UX mockups
  • Advertising copy and campaign briefs (Nevada-specific only)
  • Age restriction display templates with configurable min_age token
  • Venue and environmental signage templates (HTML/PNG renders)
  • Staff conversation scripts and FAQs (reference materials)
  • Activity statement communication mockups
Operator Provides
  • Legal review and compliance certification
  • Platform features (deposit limits, self-exclusion infrastructure)
  • Age verification technology
  • Activity statement data pipelines
  • Jurisdiction-specific mandatory verbatim text
  • Advertising pre-clearance and media buying
  • Staff training program, e-learning, and certification
  • Game-specific RTP values from their platform
  • Self-exclusion enrollment mechanics and scheme integration
  • Session tracking and monitoring systems
  • Press-ready production files from signage templates
