Messaging Library

Two Pillars. Coded for Every Touchpoint.

Every piece of Playbook content flows from two brand pillars — Open and Social. Each message carries a pillar code, tone register, and tier tag, so teams can mix, filter, and deploy the right message for any channel.

Open
No fine print
Transparency about how things actually work. The house edge, the odds, the T&Cs, the stuff nobody reads. If it affects your play, we'll tell you straight.
Social
Worth sharing
Content worth talking about. Quizzes, myth-busters, facts that make you the smartest person at the table. Share it. Challenge your friends. Start conversations.

Cultural Adaptation

One Brand. Any Market.

Playbook is language-agnostic by design — built on short key messages, universal iconography, and visual storytelling that translate across markets without losing brand DNA. Five spectrums control how the voice sounds in each locale; the visual identity and core messages stay the same.

Measurement

Built-In Testing Loop

Did the content resonate? Do players remember the key message? Does adapted content work better than the default? Two ready-to-use surveys grounded in validated instruments.

Pulse

3 items · under 1 min

Quick signal after any launch. NPS, one semantic differential, one open-ended question. Any marketing team can run this.

Full

7 items · about 2 min

Diagnostic depth. Five semantic differential pairs anchored to brand DNA, plus NPS and open-ended feedback. Use for cultural fit A/B testing.
