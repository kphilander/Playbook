Compliance

Jurisdiction-Ready. Adopt It Tomorrow.

Every jurisdiction has different RG requirements — mandatory disclosures, helpline numbers, age gates, self-exclusion language. Playbook handles this with a modular compliance system. Fork the repo, drop in your jurisdiction module, and every template updates automatically.

Complete

United States — Nevada

NGC regulatory requirements, NCPG national helpline, Nevada Council resources, age-21 mandatory language. All templates render with compliance out of the box.

Helpline: 1-800-522-4700

In Development

Canada — Ontario

iGaming Ontario (iGO) RG requirements, ConnexOntario helpline, age-19 mandatory language.

In Development

United Kingdom

Gambling Commission LCCP social responsibility codes, GambleAware requirements, age-18 mandatory language.

In Development

Australia

National Consumer Protection Framework, Gambling Help Online resources, state-by-state self-exclusion registers.

In Development

European Union

Cross-border responsible gambling framework covering common requirements across EU member states.

In Development

Macau SAR

DICJ responsible gaming requirements, local helpline and self-exclusion integration.

Suggest a jurisdiction →

Each module is a YAML file mapping local requirements to the brand’s content system — helpline numbers, mandatory disclosures, age restrictions, and self-exclusion references. Fork the repo, drop in your jurisdiction module, and every template updates automatically.
