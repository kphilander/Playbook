## What you'll be able to do after this program

- Navigate the Playbook jurisdiction module system and locate requirements for any deployed market
- Read a jurisdiction module and identify the obligation type for each messaging requirement (verbatim, obligation-based, standard-based)
- Distinguish between compliant and non-compliant advertising content using jurisdiction-specific rules
- Apply the content review rubric to new {{PROGRAM_NAME}} content before publication
- Execute a quarterly regulatory check and know when to trigger a content update
- Explain how co-branding rules and {{PROGRAM_NAME}} placement requirements work across channels

## Why recertification is every 6 months

Gambling regulations change frequently. The Playbook governance model uses quarterly regulatory checks, and compliance staff need current knowledge to execute them. The 6-month recertification cycle ensures that the people running those checks are up to date.

## What comes next

After completing Compliance, your role in the {{PROGRAM_NAME}} system is ongoing:

| Activity | Cadence |
|----------|---------|
| Quarterly regulatory check | Every 3 months — verify messaging, helplines, advertising rules, self-exclusion requirements |
| Content review | As needed — use the rubric from Module 02 for any new or updated content |
| Jurisdiction module updates | Within 30 days of any regulatory change |
| Recertification | Every 6 months |
