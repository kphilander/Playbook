## What you'll take away from this program

- A clear understanding of why {{PROGRAM_NAME}} exists and the problem it solves
- The business case for entertainment literacy: player retention, brand differentiation, and regulatory compliance
- How the white-label system works — fork once, configure in `_brand.yml`, and the entire brand cascades
- What to measure (engagement and adoption) and what not to measure (clinical outcomes)
- The governance model: who owns the brand, what the review cadence looks like, and how it scales by organization size
