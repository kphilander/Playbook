Playbook's visual system uses three core colors. [pause] Navy, the primary brand color, conveys trust and authority. Teal is the accent for interactive elements, links, and calls to action. Bold orange brings energy to Tier 1 material, but disappears completely in Tier 2.

[speaking confidently] The visual system is designed for maximum contrast and accessibility. Every color combination must meet W-CAG double-A standards. The typography uses a heading font for hierarchy and a body font for readability. Icons follow a consistent line-weight style.

[emphatically] The system is deliberately restrained. Visual noise is the enemy of player education.
