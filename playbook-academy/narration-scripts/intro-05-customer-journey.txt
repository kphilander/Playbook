Every player moves through five journey phases: Awareness, Onboarding, Active Play, Ongoing Engagement, and Support Seeking. At each phase, different Playbook content serves a different purpose. Onboarding normalizes tool use. Active Play provides session awareness. Ongoing Engagement delivers myth-busting campaigns and quiz challenges. The key insight: content appears at natural moments, not as interruptions. A deposit screen is a natural moment for budget-setting. A session reminder is a natural moment for activity awareness. Playbook adds value in context.
