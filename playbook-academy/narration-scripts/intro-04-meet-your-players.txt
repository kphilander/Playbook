Playbook defines seven player segments. General Players are your baseline — recreational, curious, responsive to odds literacy. Young Adults aged 18 to 25 are digital-native and social-first — they need myth-busting content and quiz challenges. Sports Bettors are data-driven and believe skill matters — they need vig education and parlay math. Traditional Players play lottery, bingo, and horse racing — they respond to community connection. At-Risk Players show behavioral indicators like escalating spend — they need non-judgmental tool visibility. Help Seekers are actively looking for support — they need speed and zero friction. And Friends and Family are concerned about someone else — they need conversation starters and referral resources.
