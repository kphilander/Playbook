Here's how to get started. Phase 1, your MVP, ships in 2 to 4 weeks: deploy for general players only with 6 mobile touchpoints, 4 email templates, the helpline footer, and the Game IQ Quiz as your launch vehicle. Phase 2 expands to your priority segments over months 2 to 3. Phase 3 is full deployment with all segments, campaign rotation, and staff training by month 6. Start by forking the repo, editing brand.yml with your program name and helpline numbers, and working through the Academy modules. You don't need to be an RG expert. You just need to understand the system. That's what the modules are for.
