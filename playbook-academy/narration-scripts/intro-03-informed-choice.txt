Playbook is built on the Informed Choice Model. Three premises: most people who gamble do so without problems, gambling is entertainment with a cost called the house edge, and players make better decisions when they understand the math. This means 95% of Playbook content is Tier 1 — entertainment literacy. How games work, what the odds mean, tools framed as features. The remaining 5% is Tier 2 — helplines, self-exclusion, crisis support. Both tiers are essential. But the system is designed around education, not crisis.
