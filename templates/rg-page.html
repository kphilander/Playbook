Self-Exclusion

Need some time away?

If gambling isn't fun anymore, or you want a break for any reason, self-exclusion lets you block your own access for a set period. No judgement. It's your call.

Choose your duration Options typically range from 6 months to 5 years, or permanent. Pick what feels right.

Your account is locked During the exclusion period, you can't deposit, bet, or open new accounts. Marketing stops too.

Support is still available Self-exclusion doesn't mean you're alone. Helplines and counseling remain accessible at any time.
