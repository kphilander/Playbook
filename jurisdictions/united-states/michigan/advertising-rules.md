## Michigan's advertising framework

### Hybrid approach

Michigan regulates gambling advertising through a **hybrid** framework: broad statutory standards set the floor, and administrative rules add specific requirements for interactive platforms. There is no single prescriptive advertising regulation of the Ohio (OAC 3775-16-08) type; instead, advertising obligations are distributed across the statutes and the internet gaming / sports betting rule sets.

| Layer | Source | Nature | Scope |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Statutory standards** | Lawful Internet Gaming Act (PA 152 of 2019); Lawful Sports Betting Act (PA 149 of 2019) | Standard-based | All interactive advertising (truthful/not-misleading; no targeting under-21) |
| **Promotional wagering rules** | R 432.649 (iGaming) / R 432.749 (sports betting) | Rule-based | Tournaments/contests and bonus and promotional wagering (iGaming, R 432.649); the sports-betting rule (R 432.749) additionally titles advertising and player loyalty programs |
| **Responsible gaming page rules** | R 432.654 (iGaming) / R 432.754 (sports betting) | Standard-based | RG logo, RG page, helpline message required wherever advertising drives to the platform |
| **2025 MR 9 amendments** | MGCB rulemaking, eff. 4/29/2025 | Rule-based | Reinforced no targeting (under-21, self-excluded); truthful/not-misleading |
| **Land-based** | Michigan Gaming Control and Revenue Act + MGCB-approved internal controls | Obligation-based | Detroit commercial casino advertising and in-venue signage |

### Key differences from peer states

| Area | Michigan | Ohio | New Jersey |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Approach** | Hybrid (statutory + rule) | Prescriptive (OAC 3775-16-08) | Standard-based (DGE Best Practices) + prescriptive (NJAC 13:69O) |
| **Prescribed advertising message** | None mandated (verbatim helpline string removed by 2025 MR 9) | Prevention message required in all sports ads | "Bet With Your Head, Not Over It" or comparable |
| **Helpline in ads** | RG information/helpline required for interactive (R 432.654 / R 432.754) | Required (NCPG or state helpline) | 1-800-GAMBLER prominently displayed |
| **Promotions rule** | R 432.649 / R 432.749 | Via plan (casino) | NJAC 13:69O-1.2 |
| **Self-excluded targeting** | Prohibited (2025 MR 9; marketing cessation duty) | Cease within 15 days (OAC 3772-12-04) | Removed from marketing; lists shareable for opt-out |

## Internet gaming and sports betting advertising

### Promotional wagering rules (R 432.649 / R 432.749)

Promotional conduct is governed by **R 432.649** (internet gaming) and the parallel **R 432.749** (internet sports betting). The two rules are **not identical in titled scope**: R 432.749 is titled *"Tournaments/contests, bonus and promotional wagering, advertising, and player loyalty programs,"* so for sports betting it reaches advertising and loyalty programs by its terms. R 432.649 is titled only *"Tournaments/contests and bonus and promotional wagering"* — its title does **not** include advertising or player loyalty programs, so for internet gaming, advertising obligations rest primarily on the statutory standards (PA 152) and R 432.654 rather than R 432.649's title. Both rules cover the mechanics of bonus/promotional offers.

| Requirement | Details | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Promotional wagering mechanics** | Bonus and promotional wagering must operate as described and disclosed | R 432.649 (iGaming) / R 432.749 (sports betting) |
| **Loyalty programs** | Player loyalty programs are named in R 432.749's title (sports betting); the iGaming rule R 432.649 does not title loyalty programs | R 432.749 (sports betting); compare R 432.649 (iGaming) |
| **Advertising** | Advertising is named in R 432.749's title (sports betting); for iGaming, advertising standards derive from PA 152 and R 432.654, not R 432.649's title | R 432.749 (sports betting); PA 152 + R 432.654 (iGaming) |
| **Truthful presentation** | Promotional offers and advertising must be truthful and not misleading | PA 152 / PA 149 |

> **Pin the right rule by vertical.** Use **R 432.649** for internet gaming promotions and **R 432.749** for internet sports betting promotions. They are separate, independently enforceable rules — and R 432.749's title is broader (it names advertising and player loyalty programs; R 432.649's title does not).

### Responsible gaming page requirements that attach to advertising (R 432.654 / R 432.754)

Any interactive advertising that drives a participant to the operator's platform must lead to a platform that satisfies the responsible gaming page rules. Under **R 432.654** (internet gaming) and **R 432.754** (internet sports betting), each operator must:

1. Display an MGCB-approved **responsible gaming logo** directing to the operator's responsible gaming page
2. Maintain a **responsible gaming page** accessible during active sessions that includes:
   - A prominent message informing participants of a toll-free compulsive/problem gambling helpline number (operators currently display 1-800-GAMBLER)
   - A direct link to MGCB's Compulsive/Problem Gambling website
   - Links to **other organizations based in the United States** dedicated to helping people experiencing gambling problems
   - A clear statement of the operator's responsible gaming policy and commitment

These rules are the advertising-side anchor for the helpline/RG information that should appear in or be reachable from interactive advertising.

## 2025 MR 9 advertising changes

The **2025 MR 9 rulemaking** (effective **April 29, 2025**) amended the internet gaming and sports betting rules, including **R 432.654** and **R 432.754**. For advertising, the operative points are:

| Change | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Helpline wording** | Deleted the prescribed verbatim MDHHS helpline string; rules now require only a generic prominent toll-free helpline message |
| **No targeting under-21** | Reinforced that advertising must not target individuals under 21 |
| **No targeting self-excluded** | Reinforced that advertising must not target self-excluded persons |
| **Truthful / not misleading** | Reinforced the truthful-and-not-misleading standard for advertising |

> **Source**: MGCB online gaming and sports betting rule revisions (2025 MR 9). The michigan.gov rulemaking PDF bot-blocks automated checks — *verify before merge*. Effective-date and amendment scope corroborated against the Cornell LII rule mirrors for R 432.654 and R 432.754.

## AGA Responsible Gaming Code

The **American Gaming Association (AGA)** Responsible Gaming Code of Conduct applies as a voluntary industry overlay. Michigan operators that are AGA members typically comply with both the Michigan rules and the AGA principles.

| AGA principle | Michigan requirement | Status |
|---|---|---|
| No targeting minors | No targeting under-21 (PA 152 / PA 149; 2025 MR 9) | Aligned |
| Truthful advertising | Truthful/not-misleading (PA 152 / PA 149; 2025 MR 9) | Aligned |
| Responsible gambling messaging | RG logo + RG page with helpline (R 432.654 / R 432.754) | Aligned |
| Self-exclusion compliance | Cease marketing to self-excluded individuals; prohibited-person duties (R 432.676 / R 432.776) | Aligned |

## Channel-by-channel rules

Michigan's interactive advertising obligations apply across channels; the key variables are vertical (cite R 432.654 / R 432.649 for iGaming, R 432.754 / R 432.749 for sports betting) and whether the placement can carry full RG information or must link to it.

| Channel | Key requirements | Verticals | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Broadcast (TV)** | Truthful/not-misleading; 21+; helpline/RG information; end card recommended for helpline | All | PA 152 / PA 149; R 432.654 / R 432.754 |
| **Broadcast (radio)** | Truthful/not-misleading; 21+; spoken helpline reference | All | PA 152 / PA 149 |
| **Digital (display/video)** | RG logo + RG page reachable; helpline; promotional offers per R 432.649 / R 432.749 | Interactive | R 432.654 / R 432.754; R 432.649 / R 432.749 |
| **Digital (social media)** | 21+ targeting controls; helpline/RG link; truthful promotional claims | Interactive | PA 152 / PA 149; R 432.649 / R 432.749 |
| **Print / billboard** | Truthful/not-misleading; 21+; not placed to target under-21 audiences | All | PA 152 / PA 149 |
| **Direct marketing** | No marketing to self-excluded individuals (DPL / online program) or database-listed prohibited persons; opt-out honored | All | MGCB rules; R 432.676 / R 432.776 |
| **In-venue (casino)** | RG information posted per MGCB-approved internal controls | Land-based | Michigan Gaming Control and Revenue Act |
| **In-venue (sportsbook)** | RG information and helpline visible; truthful promotional signage | Land-based / interactive | Michigan Gaming Control and Revenue Act; R 432.749 |

## Prohibited content

Michigan statutes and rules prohibit advertising that:

| Prohibition | Details | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **False or misleading claims** | About odds, winnings, or gambling outcomes | PA 152 / PA 149; 2025 MR 9 |
| **Targeting under-21** | Advertising must not target individuals under 21 | PA 152 / PA 149; 2025 MR 9 |
| **Targeting self-excluded persons** | No marketing to DPL or online self-exclusion enrollees | MGCB rules; 2025 MR 9 |
| **Targeting prohibited persons** | No marketing to involuntary prohibited persons in the Responsible Gaming Database | R 432.671 / R 432.771; duties R 432.676 / R 432.776 |
| **Promoting excessive play** | Advertising must not promote irresponsible or excessive gambling | PA 152 / PA 149 |
| **Risk-free misrepresentation** | Misleading claims about the risk-free nature of gambling | PA 152 / PA 149; 2025 MR 9 |
| **Non-conforming promotions** | Bonus/promotional offers that do not operate as disclosed | R 432.649 / R 432.749 |

## Required disclosures

### Interactive advertising

| Disclosure | Requirement | Where |
|---|---|---|
| **21+** | Age requirement communicated | All interactive ads |
| **Helpline / RG information** | Toll-free helpline message; RG page reachable (1-800-GAMBLER currently displayed) | All interactive ads / linked platform |
| **Geolocation** | Must be physically located in Michigan to participate | All interactive ads |
| **Operator identity** | Licensed operator name | All interactive ads |
| **Promotional terms** | Material terms of any bonus/promotional offer disclosed | Where promotions are advertised |

### Land-based / in-venue

| Disclosure | Requirement | Where |
|---|---|---|
| **RG information** | Problem gambling information and helpline posted per internal controls | Detroit commercial casino gaming areas |
| **Self-exclusion** | Information on the DPL available to patrons | Casino RG materials |
