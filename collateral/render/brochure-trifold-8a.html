Real Odds
The house edge — what it means for you
The house edge is the mathematical advantage built into every casino game. It's not hidden. It's how the business works. Knowing the edge helps you pick your games and set your budget.
GameHouse edge
Blackjack (basic strategy)0.5%
Craps (pass/don't pass)1.4%
European roulette2.7%
American roulette5.26%
Slots2–15%

For every $100 you wager on American roulette, the casino keeps about $5.26 over time. That's the cost of the entertainment.

House edge assumes optimal strategy where applicable.
